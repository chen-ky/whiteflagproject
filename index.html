Who are we?

The White Flag Project is a COVID-19 relief initiative by r/Malaysia . We maintain a rich, well-updated and open database of food banks and other aid resources from across Malaysia.

We invite you to use this data for any purpose which might help our nation during this period of crisis. For more information, please join our Discord or send us an email.
